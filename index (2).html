How to Play 📖

Welcome to PocketPal! 🐾

🐾 Your Pet

Keep your pet alive and happy! Stats decay over time. If all 3 critical stats (Hunger, Thirst, Health) hit zero, it's Game Over.

💰 Budget

You set category limits before starting. Earn more cash by doing chore minigames. Never let your wallet hit $0!

✨ Evolution

Reach Level 5 to evolve! Your pet's form is determined by how you cared for them — play lots + use specials for Gentle, neglect for Fierce, balance for Wise, or be inconsistent for Chaos!

🎮 Minigames

Do household chores to earn money. 6 types: Whack-a-Mole, Math Quiz, Simon Says, Memory Match, Sorting, and Tap Rhythm.

🌟 Special Tasks

Your pet has unique tasks based on species — cats need brushing, dogs need walks, dragons need flame practice! These help unlock the Gentle evolution!

📊 Grades

Get graded every 30s on Nutrition, Wellness, Engagement, Consistency, and Budget. Aim for A+!

💡 Evolution Tips

  • Gentle Soul — Play often, use special tasks, keep happiness high & neglect low
  • Fierce Warrior — Mostly ignore your pet, minimal play, lots of hunger/thirst neglect
  • Wise Elder — Balanced care: moderate everything, no extremes
  • Chaos Lord — Very inconsistent: neglect sometimes but care intensely other times
  • Stats decay faster at higher levels — check in often!
  • Doing chores early builds a financial safety net
  • Pet your companion by clicking them for a free happiness boost!
